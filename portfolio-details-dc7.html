Project Details

This assignment was focused on the use of typography and text in regards to design. The objective was to pick a quote, at least two typefaces, and create a design around the quote. I picked a quote from Alan Watts and based my design off of an eye chart. I believe the quote in the eye chart demonstrates the simplicity the quote is referencing and each line of the eye chart leads you to next part of the text.
In addition to the eye chart design, I also created a more repetitive iteration of the quote. The quote is repeated over and over as the background of the image in a smaller font size and is also the foreground of the design in a larger font and bright green.
