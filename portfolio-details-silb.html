Blog Details

This piece was actually inspired by one of my favorite midnight snacks: buttered toast. One night, I went to go make myself this wonderfully easy snack, but as the toast was toasting I realized I didn’t have any softened butter (aka counter butter). This made a two-minute quick bite turn into a very long eight minutes. I had to try to melt fridge butter onto my toast without melting it too much. Spoiler: it didn’t work and it wasn’t the same. The following morning is when I was struck with the idea to compare spreading butter on toast to spreading information. I was struggling to come up with an interesting take on the topic and I thought this analogy was perfect because of how relatable and simple it is.
