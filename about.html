Why Marketing?

Growing up, I was always very creative, yet also analytical. The message I received along the way was find a path that focused those talents in one direction or the other. This opinion was constantly reinforced as I waded my way through degree programs. Finally, at the suggestion of my parents, I looked into Marketing, and finally found my true calling. I very quickly appreciated the psychology, the creativity, the analysis of various different elements, and the dynamic landscape. I am excited to have found a way to utilize my strengths and flourish in the many opportunities that are ahead of me.
