Project Details

The goal of this assignment was to design something that would be beneficial for spending time alone. The idea of creating the "Unplug App" was that with social media and technology in today's society, we are never truly alone (even with functions like Do Not Distrub). The app will disconnect you from all forms of social media and other communication apps until reconnected. There was a bit of a safety concern, so if someone tries to text, call or email while "unplugged" a customizable automated message will be sent to them alerting the person who they are trying to reach is "unplugged."
